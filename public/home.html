About Us

Our web application aims to address the challenges faced by farmers by promoting economic growth through innovation in the agricultural sector. By integrating technology with farming, we seek to make agriculture a viable and attractive career option for the youth. Our platform offers six main functionalities designed to support farmers and encourage youth involvement in agriculture such as Farmers Community Group, Crop Disease detection, Crowd Funding, Agricultural Guidance, Agricultural Updates and Weather details.
